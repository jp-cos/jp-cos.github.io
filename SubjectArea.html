教科等を表すリソース

https://w3id.org/jp-cos/SubjectArea

詳細情報

Type
http://www.w3.org/2000/01/rdf-schema#Class
Comment
算数などの教科等を示すリソースです。元データの「教科等」列の値。学習指導要領コードの3桁目と対応します。
このリソースは次のようなURIにより表現されます。
https://w3id.org/jp-cos/学校種別・障害分類/告示年/教科名
This resource represents subject area such as mathematics. The value of the "Subject, etc." column in the original data. It corresponds to the 3th digit of the course of study code.
URIs for the resources are represented as the following forms:
https://w3id.org/jp-cos/type of school and disability category/date published/name of subject area
Example
https://w3id.org/jp-cos/UpperSecondaryDeptSNES-Intellectual/2019/2019/外国語
